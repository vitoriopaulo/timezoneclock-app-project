About This App

This Timezone Clock app allows you to manage multiple clocks for different time zones. You can add up to 7 clocks, each representing a different location.

To add a clock, simply click the "Add Clock" button and enter the name of the city (e.g., "New York" or "Bahia"). The app will automatically handle the time zone for you!
